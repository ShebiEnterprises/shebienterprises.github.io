About Shebi Enterprises

Cladding, glazing and façade execution built on site discipline, mock-ups and long-term relationships.

Our story

Shebi Enterprises began as a specialist stone and ACP site team serving Mumbai developers who needed predictable quality at height. Over two decades the scope grew into full envelope packages - curtain wall, structural glass, metal fabrication and maintenance - while the operating model stayed the same: clear drawings, documented mock-ups, and supervisors who can read interfaces before they become leaks.

We work alongside architects, façade consultants and main contractors through tender clarifications, VE options and execution sequencing. Monsoon exposure, hoist sharing and live hospitality sites are normal conditions for us; we plan buffers instead of promising impossible dates.

Values How we work

Transparency

BOQs tied to drawings, change orders in writing, and weekly photo logs for clients who cannot visit site daily.

Craft at height

Training for anchor patterns, sealant geometry and glass handling - not generic “contractor” labour pools.

After handover

Maintenance retainers and responsive call-outs because façades age; we want the elevation to stay proud.

Certifications Compliance & partners

We follow manufacturer method statements, site safety programmes aligned to main contractor rules, and third-party testing where the specification demands it. On projects targeting green rating pathways (IGBC, LEED), we align glazing and cladding interfaces with the façade consultant and MEP team for performance modelling inputs - without substituting their certification role.
