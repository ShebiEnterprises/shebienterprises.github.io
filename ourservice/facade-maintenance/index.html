Facade Maintenance

Protect your asset with scheduled inspection, cleaning and sealant renewal on every cladding type.

What is it?

Facade maintenance

Mumbai's coastal climate, monsoon rains and pollution are harsh on building facades. Regular professional maintenance extends facade life, maintains appearance and prevents costly structural repairs. Shebi Enterprises offers comprehensive facade inspection, cleaning and restoration for all cladding and glazing types.

Services include annual inspection, ACP and glass cleaning, sealant replacement, minor repairs and full restoration scopes.

Capabilities

  • Rope-access and BMU-trained crews
  • Photo reports and defect mapping
  • Sealant compatibility testing before renewal
  • Panel re-securement and gasket replacement
