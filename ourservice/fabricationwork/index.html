Fabrication Works

Precision fabrication for sub-structures, canopies, portals and balustrades that integrate with cladding.

What is it?

Metal fabrication

Metal fabrication is the backbone of any facade system - from sub-structures and support frames to canopies, entrance structures and decorative elements. Shebi Enterprises provides precision fabrication in MS (mild steel), stainless steel and aluminium.

Shop drawings, hot-dip galvanising coordination and site-fit tolerances are part of our standard workflow.

Capabilities

  • CNC-assisted cutting and welding QA
  • Galvanising and duplex coating systems
  • Trial assembly for complex nodes
  • Site welding with method statements
