Structural Glass

Frame-free glass walls where the glass carries load — dramatic transparency for lobbies and retail.

What is it?

Structural glass

Structural glass systems use the glass itself as a load-bearing element, creating seamless, frame-free glass facades. The result is a dramatic, ultra-modern appearance used in premium commercial towers, hotel lobbies and luxury retail spaces.

We work with specialist silicone and hardware partners for structural bite and redundancy per engineering.

Capabilities

  • Silicone structural glazing (SSG)
  • Fin-supported walls
  • Lobby height coordination
  • Temporary protection and handover cleaning
